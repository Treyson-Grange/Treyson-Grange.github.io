Here is the link of the project my group, and I submitted to HackUSU 2022 competition. Suprisingly made it into the final round of judging

The game, College Simulator was created in 21 hours, and the graphics, gameplay, and lack of music represents that. The game follows our protagonist as they go through their college experience. Teachers such as Erik Falor show up to throw their signature attacks at the player. If the player touches these attacks, their sleep gets depleted. The only way to get that sleep back is to get energy drinks that fly accross the screen. At the end of the game, the character is presented a piece of paper! Wow super cool! All Assets were created by my group, and the program was written in python, using pygame
